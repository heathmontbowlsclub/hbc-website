Angeling + Darts

Heathmont Anglers

The resident Heathmont Anglers Club, meet every second Wednesday of the month in the Bowls Club bar.

They organise regular fishing trips throughout the year.

To join Heathmont Anglers you just need to become a member of Heathmont Bowls Club - including our $10 yearly social membership.

Like and Follow the Heathmont Anglers on Facebook




Darts

The resident Eastern Darts Competition takes place every Thursday night during winter in the bar.

7.45pm start – approx 10pm finish.

Six players per team. Entry fee – $36 per team. Food supplied to participants.

For further details please contact Lisa Edwards on 043 852 5722, or Greg Hill on: 0425 729 272
