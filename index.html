About our Club

Set in a secluded, tranquil part of Maroondah, the Club is known locally as a 'hidden gem'. Widely admired for its stunning views of the Dandenong Ranges - and for having arguably the best bowling greens in Melbourne - the Club's motto is 'Amaroo', which is an indigenous word for a 'beautiful place'. Whether you are interested in playing bowls, socialising in our fully licensed bar, barefoot bowls parties, or booking our exclusive function room - for family or corporate events - you will find a very warm welcome here, and much, much more.
