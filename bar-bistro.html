Bar and Bistro

Bar

Your Local Pub

The Club’s fully licensed bar is the only 'pub' in Heathmont. With beers on tap, it's a popular socialising spot for our many friendly regulars who refer to it as a ‘hidden gem’.

Open 7 days a week from 3.30pm, fully licensed and extremely well stocked, you’ll find our drink prices some of the lowest around.

The bar is open to the visitors – as a Club you do need to sign in on entry – and we do offer social membership at only $10 per year. Becoming a social member enables you to get even lower drink prices, plus as we’re a Club you won’t have to ‘sign in’ whenever you visit.

With a free-to-play pool table, six dart boards, wide screen televisions and a state of the art sound system – not to mention the good company - you won’t be short of entertainment.

Our modern outdoor decking area is the ideal place to wind-down after a busy day at work or to enjoy the serene setting on a hazy summer’s afternoon. With stunning views of the Dandenong Ranges and the tranquility of the greens, you can be sure that you will find one of the best ‘beer gardens’ in Melbourne here.

In the winter the bar hosts one of the largest darts competitions in the region. We are also home to the resident Heathmont Anglers who meet here regularly and organise fishing trips through the year.

Pop in and visit us, you’ll find very warm welcome, good beer and great company.

You might also find that the drink tastes even better here knowing that as a not-for-profit community organisation all monies from bar sales go solely back into running the Club, and providing our wonderful sporting facilities.

Our bar… your local pub.
