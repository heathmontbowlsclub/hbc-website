COVID-19 Update

Update Posted: Monday 25th May 2020

Bowling Greens NOW Open for Practice!

Greens Open Daily from 9am to 5pm - Bookings Essential


From June 1 2020, the maximum ammount of Bowlers will double to 20.


Due to the ongoing COVID-19 Pandemic we require Bowling Members to reserve a 60 Minute Bowling Session when wanting to use the HBC Synthetic Green.


A booking system has been setup to ensure all HBC Bowling Members can use the clubs greens safely and affectively.

If you are planning to bowl in a group, each BOWLING MEMBER must reserve a session time before using the green.


A MAXIMUM of 10 Bowlers are allowed on the green at any one time.

Further more, only 4 Bowlers are allowed per Rink (Pairs), no spectators are allowed to watch Bowlers. (unless they are driving a bowler to and from their practice session)


To ensure all Bowling Members can take advantage on getting back on the Green, please limit your bookings to two (2) bookings per week.

No back-to-back sessions please.

It is recommended that if you do choose to bowl, we ask that you adhere to social-distancing rules, by standing 1.5 meters away from bowlers where possible.

Please refrain from shaking hands, high-fives or any contact with others.

Please refrain from handling other people’s bowls.

Please keep your hands clean by using the sanitizer readily available at the club.

Wash your hands before and after play.

If you’re feeling unwell then you should not attend the club under any circumstances.


Please consider downloading the Australian Governments COVIDsafe app.

https://www.health.gov.au/resources/apps-and-tools/covidsafe-app

Thanks for playing fair, and have fun on the green!
