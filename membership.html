Membership

Join our club!

As a growing club we are always very open to new social or bowls members - you'll find a very warm welcome here.

Membership Types

Social Member | $10 per year

Non-bowling Membership.

Access to bar without signing in, Discounted bar prices, Discounted function room hire (after a period of membership).


Junior Membership (Under 18) | $55 per year

Junior Bowling Membership.

The benifits of a Social Membership + Compete in all games, Access to greens, Coaching available, Club shirt, Sponsor discounts.


Seniors Membership (Over 80's) | $120 per year

Seniors Discounted Membership.

The benifits of a Social Membership + Compete in pennant, Access to greens, Coaching available, Club shirt, Sponsor discounts.


Full Bowls Member | $240 per year

Full Membership.

The benifits of a Social Membership + Compete in pennant, Access to greens, Coaching available, Club shirt, Sponsor discounts.
